Jefferson btwn Wyckoff/Irving block party

January 5, 2025

Join us for our annual winter block party on Jefferson Avenue between Wyckoff and Irving! This community celebration brings together neighbors for food, music, and winter activities.

We'll have several local food vendors offering special discounts, a DJ playing throughout the day, and activities for children including a snowman-building contest (weather permitting). Local businesses along the block will be offering special promotions and giveaways.

The street will be closed to traffic from 12pm to 6pm. In case of severe weather, the event will be postponed to the following weekend. Bring your friends, family, and neighbors!
